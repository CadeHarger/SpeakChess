# SpeakChess

**Play chess using just your voice!**

SpeakChess is an iOS app that lets you play a full game of chess without ever touching the screen. Speak your moves out loud (e.g. *"Knight to f3"* or *"pawn e4"*), and the app announces your opponent's replies in natural speech. It's great for when your hands are busy or if practicing blindfolded chess.

---

## How It Works

1. **Start a game** — choose to play as White or Black and pick a difficulty level, from Beginner all the way up to Master.
2. **Speak your move** — tap the microphone button and say your move in plain English. The app understands standard chess notation and natural phrasing.
3. **Listen to the response** — your AI opponent (powered by Stockfish, the strongest chess engine in the world) thinks, plays its move, and the app reads it back to you.
4. **Review your game** — after the game ends, step through every move on an interactive board and see computer analysis highlighting the best lines at each position.

## Requirements

- iPhone running iOS 17 or later
- Microphone access (required for voice input)
- Speech recognition permission (handled on first launch)
